Breakfast Recipes

Avocado Toast with Poached Egg

A simple yet delicious breakfast packed with healthy fats.

Hover to See The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice whole-grain bread
  • 1/2 avocado, mashed
  • 1 poached egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to Make:

  1. Toast the bread until golden.
  2. Spread the mashed avocado on top.
  3. Place the poached egg on the avocado toast.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Smoothie Bowl

A colorful and nutritious breakfast or snack loaded with vitamins.

Hover to See The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • Toppings: granola, fresh fruit, chia seeds

How to Make:

  1. Blend the banana, frozen berries, Greek yogurt, and almond milk until smooth.
  2. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  3. Top with granola, fresh fruit, and chia seeds.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Greek Yogurt Parfait

Layers of creamy Greek yogurt, sweet honey, fresh fruit, and crunchy granola.

Hover to See The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup fresh fruit (such as berries, sliced banana, or diced mango)

How to Make:

  1. In a glass or bowl, add a layer of Greek yogurt.
  2. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup.
  3. Add a layer of granola.
  4. Add a layer of fresh fruit.
  5. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of fresh fruit on top.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Chia Seed Pudding

A nutritious pudding made with chia seeds soaked in almond milk, topped with fresh berries.

Hover to See The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Toppings: fresh berries, sliced banana, nuts, granola, etc.

How to Make:

  1. In a bowl or jar, combine chia seeds, almond milk, honey (or maple syrup), and vanilla extract (if using).
  2. Stir well to ensure the chia seeds are evenly distributed.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight, allowing the chia seeds to absorb the liquid and form a pudding-like consistency.
  4. Before serving, give the pudding a good stir to break up any clumps.
  5. Top with your favorite toppings such as fresh berries, sliced banana, nuts, or granola.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
