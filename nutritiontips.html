Myths vs Facts

‚úÖ Fact: Healthy fats like avocados, nuts, and olive oil are essential for brain function, hormone production, and overall health. It's excess calories (from any food) that contribute to weight gain, not just fat.

‚úÖ Fact: Not all carbs are bad! Whole grains, fruits, and vegetables provide fiber, energy, and essential nutrients. The real issue is refined carbs (like white bread and sugary snacks), which can cause blood sugar spikes.

‚úÖ Fact: Carbohydrates are not the enemy! Whole carbs, such as whole grains, fruits, and vegetables, provide essential energy and fiber. The problem arises with refined carbs like white bread, pastries, and sugary drinks, which can lead to weight gain due to excess calorie consumption and blood sugar spikes.

‚úÖ Fact: Skipping meals can actually backfire! When you skip meals, your metabolism slows down to conserve energy, and you may experience increased hunger and cravings, leading to overeating later. Instead, focus on balanced meals throughout the day with proteins, healthy fats, and fiber to support steady energy and healthy weight management
